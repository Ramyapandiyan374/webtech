Chocolate Chip Cookies

Prep Time15 minutes

Cook Time10 minutes

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 0F(180 0C)
  2. Mix flour and sugar in a bowl
  3. Add chocolate chips and stir well
  4. Bake for 10 minnutes or until golden brown

Ingredients

Ingredient Quantity
Flour 2 cups
Sugar 1 cup
Chololate chips 1 cup
